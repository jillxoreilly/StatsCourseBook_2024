Fancy joint plots#

You can use the function sns.jointplot that we saw before (scatterplot plus the two marginal histograms) to make some fancy plots!

Let’s revisit the relationship between Tmin and Tmax

sns.jointplot(data=weather, x='Tmin', y='Tmax')

That scatterplot is too crowded. How about a 2D histogram in which shading indicates the count of datapoints in each square?

sns.jointplot(data=weather, x='Tmin', y='Tmax', kind='hist')

Or a join kde plot? Change ‘kind’ to kde above

Or how about a hex plot? Change ‘kind’ to hex above

You can find many more nice examples in the Seaborn Gallery - why not try some out? If you click on any of the pictures of plots you get the code snippet for the plot.
